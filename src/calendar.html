Instructions

  • Use the "View Switcher" button to toggle between monthly and weekly views. This button looks like a calendar.
  • Click the left arrow button to navigate to the previous month or week.
  • Click the right arrow button to navigate to the next month or week.
  • To add an event, click the "Add Event" button and fill out the form.
  • To add a goal, click the "Add Goal" button and fill out the form.
  • Click on a specific day's "add goal/event" button in the calendar itself to pre-load the form with that date when adding an event or goal.
  • Click on an event in the calendar to view it.
  • Click on a goal in the calendar to view it.
  • Use the form on the home page to choose which country to load holidays for in the calendar.
